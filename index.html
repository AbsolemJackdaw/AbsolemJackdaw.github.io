About Absolem Jackdaw

Absolem Jackdaw,

A name that ripened over the years, it is a mashup of a historically accurate ancestor of his irl name and his favourite bird. Absolem has also been known troughout the years as ArtixAllMighty and Subaraki (formerly senpaisubaraki).

Absolem has wanted to develop games ever since he got his hands on his favourite game, Ratchet And Clank. Passing trough an artistic career first related to an ideal of designing, in 2012 he got his hands on Minecraft and the community wide-spread tutorials that came with it for modding and Java, and has been learning and coding in Java ever since.

After programming for hobby for years, in 2021 he got his hands on a scholar ship in Belgium at the PXL in Hasselt to get a degree in IT and does his very best to learn a plethora of new languages and concepts.

Projects

Absolem has his hands full with school and his Minecraft Modding projects.

All projects and side projects can be found on his Github and working builds of mods on CurseForge.
