About Absolem Jackdaw

Absolem Jackdaw,

A name that ripened over the years, it is a mashup of a historically accurate ancestor of my irl name and my favourite bird. I have also been known troughout the years as ArtixAllMighty and Subaraki (formerly senpaisubaraki).

I have always wanted to develop games ever since I got my hands on what could be my all time favourite game, Ratchet And Clank. Passing trough an artistic career first, related to an ideal of designing, in 2012 I got my hands on Minecraft and the community wide-spread tutorials that came with it for modding and Java, and have been learning and coding in Java ever since.

After programming for hobby for years, in 2021 I got my hands on a scholarship in Belgium at the PXL in Hasselt to get a degree in IT and do my very best to learn a plethora of new languages and concepts.
